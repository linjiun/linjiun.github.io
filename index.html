About

Linjiun is currently an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science and Information Management at Soochow University. He is interested in using machine learning, especially deep neural networks, to solve problems in information retrieval and patent data mining. Before joining Soochow University in 2023, he had worked in the industry for about fifteen years and accumulated rich experience in the research and development of machine learning products. He is the first inventor on several patents and the first author on several IEEE papers.

    Patent Data Mining
